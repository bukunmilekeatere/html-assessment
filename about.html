ABOUT


The Story.

Power to the Reader is an online book review network that connects us together because of our love of books. Our mission is to share that love for books to as many people as we can reach. To provide inspiration, and motivation to our readers by giving personal insight needed to help find their next read or possibly favorite book. Basically, this serves as a hub for bookworms. Enjoy!


The Team.

Hi there! I am Bukunmi. I'm the creator and manager of this website. I am deeply passionate about reading and the personal differences in interpretations of books. Power to the Reader is a means for me to communicate analysis of different books, author bios, and just general book talk. It's also a way for me to know other's inputs, like i stated earlier it is always nice to hear other people's take on things. You can view this as an online version of a book club. I believe you can inspire and be inspired today, just browse with an open mind. Welcome to Power to the Reader!
