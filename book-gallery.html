BOOK GALLERY


I highly suggest reading this book titled Watching You.

The author of this book is named Lisa Jewel.

Watching You is a thriller, but more specifically a psychological thriller.

This book will make you feel suspenceful and intrigued.

I rate this book a 4/5.

Watching You will have you hooked from the very beginning.


Everything I Never Told You is a wonderful book that deserves a read.

The author of this book is Celeste Ng.

Everything I Never Told You is another psychological thriller.

In a very short summary, this book will make you sad.

I rate this book a 4/5.

Everything I Never Told You is a brilliantly rendered story.


Eleanor and Park is a beautiful novel.

The author of this book is Rainbow Rowell.

Eleanor and Park is a romance book.

This book will make you feel all the emotions.

I rate this book a 3/5.

Eleanor and Park is a classic love story.


The Fault In Our Stars is a devistating novel.

The author of this book is John Green.

The Fault In Our Stars is a romance book.

This heart-wrenching book will make you cry.

I rate this book a 4.5/5.

The Fault In Our Stars is a very well written story.


We Were Liars follows a sad and tragic story-line.

The author of this book is E.Lockhart.

We Were Liars is a psychological thriller.

This book will leave your jaw wide with shock.

I rate this book a 3/5.

We Were Liars has a different literature style compared to other generic novels.


The Selection is a delightful read.

The author of this book is Kiera Cass.

The Selection is a mix between romance and dystopian fiction.

This book will make you believe in love, you are sure to catch yourself blushing a few times.

I rate this book a 4/5.

The Selection pulls on all the heartstrings.
